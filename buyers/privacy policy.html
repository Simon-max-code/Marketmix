Privacy Policy

Your privacy and trust are very important to us at MarketMix.

Welcome to MarketMix. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website, services, and mobile applications.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address when you register or place an order.
  • Payment Information: Payment card details or account information (securely processed via trusted third-party gateways).
  • Usage Data: Pages you visit, time spent on site, clicks, and actions for analytics purposes.
  • Cookies: To improve user experience and personalize content.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us to:

  • Process your orders and deliver your products.
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Send you updates, promotions, and offers (you can unsubscribe anytime).
  • Improve our services, user experience, and website performance.

3. Protecting Your Data

We use advanced encryption, secure servers, and access control to safeguard your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. However, we may share it with:

  • Delivery partners to ensure your orders reach you.
  • Payment processors for transaction verification.
  • Legal authorities if required by law or fraud investigations.

5. Your Rights

You can access, update, or delete your account information at any time by visiting your account settings. You may also contact us to withdraw consent for marketing emails.

6. Cookies Policy

MarketMix uses cookies to improve functionality and analyze traffic. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last Updated” date.
